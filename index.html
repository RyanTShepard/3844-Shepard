Introduction

My name is Ryan Shepard. I have no previous experience in digital writing. I hope that with the help of this course I will have a better understanding of how digital writing works and how to properly use and implement it in my own works.

I'm part of the track team here at Virginia Tech, which takes up a lot of my time. I am an english major, so I end up spending a lot of time reading and writing. In my free time I like to play video games, play sports with my friends, and do stuff outdoors. During the summer, I help out at camps and work with kids. My work in the community is something that I consider the work of a VT shaped student because community outreach and furthering the future of others is a key concept of the VT shaped student and Virginia Tech's motto of "ut prosim".

An experiment.
